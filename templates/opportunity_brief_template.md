ask # Opportunity Brief

## What’s the problem?
_(One clear sentence. No features. No tech.)_

## Who is affected?
_(Roles, how many users and where they sit in the workflow.)_

## What evidence proves it’s real?
*(Metrics, Quotes or observed behavior, any failed workaround patterns)*

## What is the impact of the problem? 
_(Time, cost, risk, morale, revenue, compliance — quantify if possible.)_

## Why now?
_(Trigger: regulatory change, scaling pressure, system sunset, competitive threat, strategic timing.)_

## What happens if we do nothing?
_(Show decay trajectory or missed upside.)_

## How will we know we solved it? 
_(Success criteria — outcome metrics only, no feature hints.)_
