# Definition

## Who are we building for?
*(Specific roles, volume, and where they sit in the workflow, no generic "users.")*
## What problem are we solving for them?
*(One crisp statement of the pain or inefficiency, no solution language, no tech nouns.)*
## How do we know it's a real problem?
*(Quant + qual evidence: data trends, error rates, time/cost waste, observed patterns, quotes.)*
## How do we know it's an urgent problem?
*(Quant + qual evidence: data trends, error rates, time/cost waste, observed patterns, quotes.)*
## What is the impact of the problem?
*(Time, cost, risk, morale, revenue, compliance — quantify if possible.)*
## What happens if we do nothing?
*(Show decay trajectory or missed upside.)*
## What's our proposed solution (product)?
*(High-level concept only: form factor, boundaries, non-goals, no implementation or architecture.)*
## How will we measure success?
*(Outcome-based metrics: time saved, accuracy improved, throughput, fewer handoffs, better client experience.)*
## How and when will we ship it?
*(Approach to delivery: MVP boundaries, sequencing, key dependencies, target date or release phases.)*
